About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on preparing professionals for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles.

Through collaboration with academic partners and real-world industry projects, IDR equips students and professionals with the skills required to manage risk in highly regulated environments.

Our Services

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps designed for students and professionals to build digital risk expertise.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing digital risk models, research initiatives, and AI governance frameworks.

Advisory Services

Helping organizations implement frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.

Community

IDR supports a growing community of students, early-career professionals, and industry practitioners who want to develop expertise in digital and cyber risk.

Our programs focus on sectors such as financial services, technology, and critical infrastructure, helping professionals upskill and adapt to the evolving digital risk landscape.
